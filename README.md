## 🧠 Business Analysis Case Studies

### 1. Campus Student Marketplace — Student Online Mall (SOM)
- **Type:** Independent Case Study
- **Tools:** Stakeholder Interviews, AS-IS/TO-BE Process Mapping, 
BPMN (Draw.io), Requirements Documentation, Figma (UI Prototyping)
- **The Problem:**
  - Campus commerce at TUT Soshanguve relied entirely on 
  fragmented WhatsApp groups causing message fatigue, zero 
  stock visibility and a high-friction manual payment process 
  that created an "awkward waiting moment" during every 
  transaction
- **Highlights:**
  - Conducted stakeholder interviews with student sellers and 
  performed ethnographic research as a student buyer to map 
  the complete AS-IS purchase journey
  - Identified 5 critical friction points including manual 
  beneficiary setup as the highest drop-off risk
  - Designed Student Online Mall — a centralized marketplace 
  with real-time inventory, automated booking, academic 
  schedule protection and integrated payments
  - Projected outcome: 60% reduction in checkout time through 
  QR-code payment integration
  - Produced full BPMN AS-IS process diagram, functional and 
  non-functional requirements documentation and 5-screen 
  Figma prototype
- **Deliverables:** [Case Study PDF](link) | [Prototype Screens](link) 
| [Presentation](link)

### 2. Institutional Policy Gap Analysis — Universal Residence Storage
- **Type:** Independent Initiative — Real World Outcome
- **Tools:** Policy Document Analysis, Gap Analysis, Formal 
Proposal Writing, Stakeholder Engagement
- **The Problem:**
  - TUT residence administration withdrew storage services 
  from Sosh5 Residence without policy justification, creating 
  an unreasonable burden on students from other provinces 
  during the June recess period
- **Highlights:**
  - Conducted a deep-dive audit of TUT Residence Rules and 
  Administrative Processes and identified Rule 8.7/5.13.6 — 
  a clause that explicitly permitted storage, directly 
  contradicting the administration's position
  - Authored a formal evidence-based proposal including an 
  interim measure strategy and a Universal Storage Policy 
  recommendation
  - Engaged the SRC through proper governance channels 
  independently, navigating a multi-level institutional structure
  - **Outcome: Storage was successfully reinstated for Sosh5 
  Residence students following formal submission of the proposal**
- **Deliverables:** [Full Proposal Document](link)
